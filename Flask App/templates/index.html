X-Ray Image Classifier

Note:

1. Input image must be an X-ray image only.
2. Submitting random images other than X-rays will give a irrelevant and nonsensical output.
3. Image should be in png, gif, or jpeg format.
4. X-ray should be of front view of the chest.
5. This app is designed to distinguish between COVID, Pneumonia (other viral/bacterial), and Normal. Submitting X-ray images of other diseases will result in a faulty output.
6. If Pneumonia is detected, this app will further distinguish the category of Pneumonia (other Viral or Bacterial).A probability value will be displayed which denotes the likelihood of the category of Pneumonia.
